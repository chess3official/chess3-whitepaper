## Step 2: Fund Your Wallet with SOL

You need SOL (Solana's native cryptocurrency) to mint your NFT and pay for transaction fees.

### How Much SOL Do I Need?

**For Presale Mint Pass:**
- **Mint pass price**: 0.5 SOL
- **Transaction fees**: ~0.01 SOL
- **Recommended**: Have at least 0.55 SOL in your wallet

**For Public Mint:**
- **NFT price**: 2 SOL (FREE if you have a mint pass!)
- **Transaction fees**: ~0.01 SOL
- **Recommended**: Have at least 2.05 SOL in your wallet (or just 0.01 SOL with mint pass)

### Where to Buy SOL

**Centralized Exchanges (Easiest for beginners):**

1. **Coinbase**
   - Create account at coinbase.com
   - Complete KYC verification
   - Buy SOL with credit card or bank transfer
   - Withdraw to your Phantom wallet address

2. **Binance**
   - Sign up at binance.com
   - Verify your identity
   - Purchase SOL
   - Withdraw to your wallet

3. **Kraken**
   - Register at kraken.com
   - Complete verification
   - Buy SOL
   - Send to your Phantom address

**Other Options:**
- **Crypto.com**
- **FTX** (if available in your region)
- **Moonpay** (direct purchase in Phantom)

### How to Withdraw SOL to Your Wallet

1. **Copy Your Wallet Address**
   - Open Phantom
   - Click "Receive"
   - Copy your Solana address (starts with a letter/number string)

2. **Withdraw from Exchange**
   - Go to your exchange
   - Find "Withdraw" or "Send"
   - Select SOL
   - Paste your Phantom address
   - Choose Solana network (not SPL or other)
   - Enter amount
   - Confirm withdrawal

3. **Wait for Transfer**
   - Usually takes 1-5 minutes
   - Check your Phantom wallet
   - You'll see your SOL balance update

## Step 4: Mint Your Chessalienz: Pawnz

### Understanding the Presale

Chess3 offers a **two-phase mint**:

**Phase 1: Presale Mint Pass**
- **Price**: 0.5 SOL per mint pass
- **Supply**: 1,000 mint passes
- **Benefit**: Mint 1 NFT for FREE during public launch
- **Savings**: 2 SOL per NFT!

**Phase 2: Public Mint**
- **Price**: 2 SOL per NFT
- **Mint pass holders**: FREE (mint pass is burned)
- **Supply**: 5,000 NFTs total

### Minting Process

1. **Check Current Price**
   - The current mint price is displayed prominently
   - Price updates automatically every 60 seconds (if no mints occur)

2. **Select Quantity**
   - Use the + and - buttons
   - Maximum 10 per wallet
   - Total cost is shown below

3. **Review Your Purchase**
   - Verify the quantity
   - Check the total SOL amount
   - Ensure you have enough SOL (including fees)

4. **Click "MINT NOW"**
   - The mint button will activate
   - Click once and wait

5. **Approve Transaction in Phantom**
   - Phantom popup will appear
   - Review the transaction details
   - Check the SOL amount
   - Click "Approve"

6. **Wait for Confirmation**
   - Transaction is processing
   - Usually takes 5-30 seconds
   - Do NOT close the page
   - Do NOT click mint again

7. **Success!**
   - You'll see a success message
   - Your NFT(s) are now in your wallet
   - Transaction link will be provided

## Troubleshooting

### "Insufficient Funds" Error
- **Problem**: Not enough SOL in wallet
- **Solution**: Add more SOL (remember transaction fees)

### "Transaction Failed"
- **Problem**: Network congestion or error
- **Solution**: Wait 30 seconds and try again
- Check Solana status: https://status.solana.com

### "Wallet Won't Connect"
- **Problem**: Browser or extension issue
- **Solution**: 
  - Refresh the page
  - Disable other wallet extensions
  - Try a different browser
  - Clear cache and cookies

### "NFT Not Showing in Wallet"
- **Problem**: Metadata not loaded yet
- **Solution**: 
  - Wait 5-10 minutes
  - Refresh Phantom
  - Check on Solscan with your wallet address

### "Already Minted Maximum"
- **Problem**: You've reached the 10 NFT per wallet limit
- **Solution**: Use a different wallet or wait for secondary market
